Orange Stuffed Brownies

"Orange stuffed brownies are an elite sweet treat -

they're perfect for the festive season. These brownies are fudgey, egg-free, and oozing

with a tangy, orange-curd filling, what's not to love?"

Ingredients:

Orange Curd Filling:

  • 500ml fresh orange juice
  • Zest of one orange
  • 50g caster sugar
  • 35g cornflour
  • 100ml water
  • 40g condensed milk

Brownie Batter:

Dry Ingredients

  • 50g dark chocolate, chopped
  • 225g self-raising flour
  • 50g cocoa powder
  • 125g caster sugar
  • Pinch of salt

Wet Ingredients

  • 350g condensed milk
  • 100 ml milk
  • 100 ml oil (sunflower, olive, etc.)
  • 130g dark chocolate, melted

Steps/Methods:

Make the orange curd:

  1. Mix cornflour and water
  2. Set aside when combined
  3. Add juice, zest, and sugar in pan
  4. Heat on a medium heat
  5. Let sugar dissolve
  6. Cook further for 3-5 minutes
  7. Pour in cornflour liquid
  8. Whisk curd continously
  9. Stop whisking when it thickens
  10. Let curd cook for 4-5 minutes
  11. Add condensed milk, mix
  12. Turn off heat, let curd cool
  13. Line 13x9 pan with cling wrap
  14. Pour curd into baking pan
  15. Spread curd well
  16. Place pan in freezer
  17. Let curde freeze fully

Top tip: When curd has frozen, remove the baking pan, but leave the frozen curd sheet in the freezer - you'll need the pan for baking brownies later.

Prepare the brownie batter:

  1. Mix flour, salt, cocoa, and sugar
  2. Add wet ingredients, mix
  3. Stir in chopped chocolate

Prepare stuffed brownies:

  1. Heat oven to 160F
  2. Line pan with parchment
  3. Pour half of batter in pan
  4. Spread mixture well
  5. Put frozen curd sheet on top
  6. Pour on remaining batter
  7. Spread batter, cover curd
  8. Bake for around 30 minutes
  9. Check with a skewer through center
  10. Skewer skewer should come out clean
  11. Let brownies cool fully in pan
  12. Slice into pieces and enjoy
