Cajun Chicken Pasta

"This is not authentic Cajun pasta, this is a very loose interpretation, but it still tastes damn good. I hope you all love it!"

Ingredients:

  • 1 small onion, diced
  • Half a red pepper, diced
  • 6 medium mushrooms, sliced
  • 6 garlic cloves, minced (measure with your heart)
  • 3 tbsp tomato paste
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 3/4 cup cream (whatever you have on hand. I used half and half)
  • Pasta
  • Cajun Seasoning
  • Paprika
  • Cayenne Pepper
  • Parsley Flakes
  • 2 chicken breasts
  • Panko breadcrumbs
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tbsp flour

Steps/Methods:

  1. Saute onions, peppers, mushrooms, and garlic till soft. Add tomato paste and cook for 2 minutes
  2. Lower heat to low. Add chicken broth and cream. Add seasonings (measure with your heart, I put a lot to get that strong Cajun flavor). Simmer on low until thick.
  3. Boil whatever pasta you like. Drain and set aside.
  4. Slice chicken breasts into smaller cutlets and pound out to even thickness
  5. Beat egg in a bowl and mix in flour. On a separate plate add panko breadcrumbs (enough to coat all the chicken) and Cajun seasoning.
  6. Dip chicken in egg mixture then breadcrumbs. Pan fry in your choice of oil (I shallow fried, but you can deep fry or air fry) until crispy and cooked through
  7. If sauce is not thick enough, make a cornstarch slurry and let it cook together for a few minutes. Add pasta and mix well.
