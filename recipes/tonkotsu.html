Tonkotsu Ramen

Tonkotsu Ramen:

"Ramen, or more exactly, tonkotsu ramen, is the best thing to make at home.

Top everything with a ramen egg and some chashu pork slices, and you'll be in ramen heaven in no time.

Anyone can make this taste-buds exploding, umami-abundant, bowl of noodles... even if you live in Alaska.

I know it's not the traditional way, but look at the bright side - now you can make RAMEN (sort of)!"

Ingredients:

Note: Everywhere you see vinegar - preferably, use apple cider or rice vinegar, as those don't have as harsh of a flavor as regular vinegar.

Broth:

  • 3 pounds of pig feet - or any bones and skins from large animals (pork, cow, deer, goat, moose, etc)
  • A bunch of green onions
  • 2 yellow onions
  • 1 garlic bulb
  • 6 inch knob of ginger
  • 1 red onion
